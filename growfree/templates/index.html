Who We Are & Why

As a group of first-generation, low-income, and international students, we decided to work together to solve one of our biggest challenges: the struggle of finding financially accessible programs, internships, competitions, extracurriculars, scholarships, exam prep materials, and more.
By utilizing our abilities in both front-end and back-end, we have developed a website that contain regularly updated resources and opportunities that range from free programs to educational articles to voluntary mentors.

Opening Doors to Opportunities: We facilitate students' access to newly added high-quality free opportunities that have the potential to ignite growth in their educational years. These opportunities include summer programs, competitions, internships, fellwships, research, scholarships and many more.
Improving Students' Academic Abilities by providing various free resources for both international and USA students to help them in their exam prep, college search, finfinancial aid, research, and competitons prep materials.
Guiding Students Financially: GrowFree provides a reliable source for students to learn and find free courses about financial literacy, scholarship navigation, and more as it represents one of the biggest struggles that faces them in the college admissions process.
